Henri Coanda

Inventor of Jet Engine

Henri Coanda`s Timeline:

  • 1886 - born in Bucharest, Romania.
  • 1896 - student at St. Sava highschool.
  • 1899 - he was sent by his father at the age of 13 at the military highscool.
  • 1903 - finish the highschool with the rang of seargent major and goes to School of Artillery, Engineering and Marine from Bucharest.
  • 1904 - detached to an artillery regiment from Germany - Technische Hochschule from Berlin.
  • 1905 - build a rocket plane for the romanian army.
  • 1907 - 1908 - followed the University la Liège form Belgium and the tehnical institute Montefiore.
  • 1908 - returns in Romania and he is framed as an active officer in the Artillery Regiment 2.
  • 1909 - goes in France and enroll at the Superior School of Aeronautics and construction.
  • 1910 - graduate the School as head of the first class of aircraft engineers.
  • 1910 - with the support of Gustave Eiffel and the scientist Paul Painlevé, conducted aerodynamic experiments, and he build in the workshop of Joachim Caproni the first aircraft powered reactive, actually a jet plane without propeller named Coanda-1910. During a test flight in december 1910 on the Issy-les-Moulineaux airport from paris, the machine got out of control and got hit by a wall and go fire.
  • 1911 - 1914 - worked as technical director at aviation plants in Bristol - England and built high performance aircraft propeller.
  • 1916 - build an pathfinder.
  • 1934 - get a french patent for the Coanda effect.
  • 1969 - returns permanently in Romania as creative director of the Institute of Scientific and Technical.
  • 1970 - he becomes a member of the Romanian Academy.
  • 1972 - dies at the age of 86.
